[size=5][b]Seatruck Jukebox[/b][/size]

Bring the jukebox along for the ride. Build a compact [b]Mini Jukebox[/b] and [b]Mini Speakers[/b] inside your Seatruck and listen to the music tracks you've unlocked from Jukebox Disks — with a hotkey to control playback from anywhere inside a Seatruck or base.

It also re-enables [b]building inside the Seatruck[/b] in general — a BepInEx port, updated for the current game, of BluesKutya's [i]Build In Seatruck[/i].

[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Mini Jukebox[/b] — a 25%-scale buildable jukebox that fits the Seatruck. Plays your unlocked tracks through the game's own jukebox engine, with the normal play / pause / next / shuffle / repeat / volume screen.
[*][b]Mini Speaker[/b] — a 50%-scale speaker so the music is clear inside the cab.
[*][b]Build In Seatruck[/b] — place buildables inside Seatruck segments; they ride with the vehicle. Detach/reattach works.
[*][b]Playback hotkey[/b] (while in a Seatruck or base): [b]hold[/b] (default R) to start/stop, [b]tap[/b] to skip to the next track.
[*][b]In-game options[/b] — rebind the hotkey, long-press timing, cheap recipe, unlock toggle, default shuffle, notifications.
[/list]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]
[list=1]
[*]Install the BepInEx pack and Nautilus.
[*]Extract the download so the file lands at [font=Courier New]BepInEx/plugins/SeatruckJukebox/SeatruckJukebox.dll[/font].
[*]Launch the game.
[/list]

[size=4][b]Usage[/b][/size]
The mini items unlock alongside the vanilla Jukebox (or tick "Unlock buildables at start" in the mod options). Build them with the Habitat Builder inside your Seatruck, then click the jukebox screen or use the hotkey (hold = play/stop, tap = next).
