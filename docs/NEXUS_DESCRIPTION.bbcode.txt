A [b]Subnautica: Below Zero[/b] mod with two headline features:

[list]
[*][b]🛠️ Build In Seatruck[/b] — use the Habitat Builder to place buildables [i]inside[/i] your Seatruck. Lockers, fabricators, planters, decorations — they ride with the vehicle, and detaching/reattaching modules works as expected. This is a BepInEx port, [b]updated for the current game[/b], of BluesKutya's [i]Build In Seatruck[/i].
[*][b]🎵 Mini Jukebox & Speakers[/b] — compact, Seatruck-sized buildables that play the music tracks you've unlocked from Jukebox Disks, plus a hotkey to control playback on the move.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Features[/b][/size]

[size=4][b]🛠️ Build In Seatruck[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Place buildables inside any Seatruck segment with the Habitat Builder.
[*]Everything parents to the segment, so it [b]rides with the Seatruck[/b] as you drive.
[*]Detach and reattach modules freely — your builds stay put.
[*]Build inside the [b]cab itself[/b], not just the modules — the original mod only allowed modules.
[*]Normal base and world building are completely untouched (the Seatruck handling is scoped to when you're actually standing in a Seatruck).
[*]You can also build inside the Seatruck dock room.
[/list]

[size=4][b]🎵 Mini Jukebox & Speakers[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Mini Jukebox[/b] — a 25%-scale buildable jukebox that fits the Seatruck. Plays your unlocked tracks through the game's own jukebox engine, with the normal in-world play / pause / next / shuffle / repeat / volume screen.
[*][b]Mini Speaker[/b] — a 50%-scale speaker so the music is clear inside the cramped cab.
[*]Very liberal placement collision on both, so they're easy to fit.
[/list]

[size=4][b]⌨️ Playback hotkey[/b][/size]
While inside a Seatruck or base:
[list]
[*][b]Hold[/b] the key (default [b]R[/b]) → start / stop playback.
[*][b]Tap[/b] the key → skip to the next track (with a "Now playing" toast).
[/list]

[size=4][b]⚙️ In-game options (Nautilus Mod Options)[/b][/size]
Rebind the hotkey, tune long-press timing, cheap recipe, unlock toggle, default shuffle, and notifications.

[size=5][b]Usage[/b][/size]

[size=4][b]Building in the Seatruck[/b][/size]
Get inside a Seatruck, equip the [b]Habitat Builder[/b], and build as you normally would in a base. The placement ghost turns green inside the cab and attached modules; whatever you place rides along when you drive.

[size=4][b]The jukebox[/b][/size]
[list=1]
[*][b]Unlock the mini buildables.[/b] By default the Mini Jukebox & Mini Speaker unlock alongside the vanilla Jukebox (find Jukebox Disks / scan Jukebox fragments). Prefer them from the start? Tick [b]"Unlock buildables at start"[/b] in the mod options and restart.
[*][b]Build[/b] a Mini Jukebox (and a Mini Speaker or two) in your Seatruck — they share the vanilla Jukebox's build-menu category.
[*][b]Play music:[/b] walk up and click the jukebox's screen, [b]or[/b] use the hotkey — hold R to start/stop, tap R to skip (active while inside a Seatruck or base).
[/list]
Songs are the ones you've unlocked from Jukebox Disks — the mod uses the game's own unlocked-track playlist, so there's nothing extra to manage.

[line]

[size=5][b]Configuration[/b][/size]
[b]Options → Mod Options → Build In Seatruck Plus[/b] in-game.
[list]
[*][b]Playback hotkey[/b] (default R) — play/stop (hold) and next (tap), in Seatruck/base.
[*][b]Long-press seconds[/b] (0.50) — how long to hold before it counts as play/stop.
[*][b]Cheap recipe[/b] (off) — build the mini items for 1 Titanium [i](restart required)[/i].
[*][b]Unlock buildables at start[/b] (off) — make the mini items available immediately [i](restart required)[/i].
[*][b]New jukeboxes start in shuffle[/b] (on) — sets shuffle when playback starts via the hotkey.
[*][b]Show "Now playing" notifications[/b] (on) — on-screen track name when a song starts/skips.
[/list]
